ABOUT ACCENTUS

OUR MISSION

The mind-pounding stress of college applications and standardized testing basically sums up all the feelings of high schoolers in this day and age. Along with actually acing the tests or writing their college applications, students are expected to keep up with dozens of deadlines to keep track of their progress.

Applying to colleges has become an even more daunting task as more students pursue higher education and apply to more colleges. Competition continues to increase both between students and within the college application process.

Accentus aims to decrease the stress associated with the college readiness process by eliminating the unneeded stress from keeping track of the various deadlines involved. Students will be able to avoid paying late fees for tests or secure a seat in their preferred testing location. Students can keep the seemingly random deadlines discrete and circumvent missing application deadlines. We all know that applying to colleges causes a lot of stress, but there's no reason for deadlines to be one of the major stressors.

OUR TEAM

MEET TEAM ACCENTUS.

Mansi, Megan, and Abi. The three of us met at an entrepreneurship summer program in 2018 in the beautiful city of Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. From across the globe, we all felt the struggles of preparing for colleges. We set out to crate a product that guided students through the college readiness and application processes.

We have all firsthand witnessed the stressors of college applications and would hate for others to have to deal with it as well. We decided to create this platform to help students like us avoid the these struggles.
